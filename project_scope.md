## Fastrack Driving Institute Website Specification

Build a **modern static website** for Fastrack Driving Institute using Next.js and React.
The website should visually represent a professional driving school and focus on trust, safety, and student success.
The design theme should include driving-related visuals such as roads, cars, steering wheels, traffic signs, and driving lessons.

### Color Theme

Use the institute’s brand colors as the primary palette.

Primary Green: `#1FA84A`
Primary Orange: `#F58220`

Supporting colors:
Dark Green: `#0F6D2F`
Light Green: `#EAF8EF`
Soft Orange: `#FFF3E8`
Dark Text: `#1F2937`
Light Background: `#F9FAFB`

Design style should be modern, clean, friendly, and clearly themed around a driving institute.

### Website Sections

**Navbar**

* Sticky navigation bar
* Links: Home, About, Courses, Gallery, Testimonials, Contact
* CTA button: *Enroll Now*
* White background with green accents and orange CTA button

**Hero Section**

* Large landing section with a driving-related background (road, car, or instructor teaching a student)
* Headline: “Learn Driving with Confidence”
* Subtext describing professional training and safe driving education
* Buttons: “Start Learning” and “Contact Us”

**About Section**

* Brief description of the institute
* Highlights such as experienced instructors, modern vehicles, high pass rate, and flexible schedules

**Courses Section**
Display training programs such as:

* Beginner Driving Course
* Advanced Driving Training
* License Test Preparation

Each course should be shown as a card containing an icon, short description, duration, and enroll button.

**Statistics Section**
Show achievement metrics with icons and animated counters:

* Total students trained
* Pass rate
* Years of experience
* Number of training vehicles

Example values:

* 5000+ Students Trained
* 98% Pass Rate
* 10+ Years Experience
* 15+ Training Vehicles

**Gallery Section**
Image grid showing:

* driving lessons
* training vehicles
* students practicing
* instructor guidance
* successful license moments

Images should have hover zoom effects.

**Testimonials Section**
Carousel or card layout with student reviews including:

* student name
* photo
* review text
* star rating

Example testimonial:
“Great instructors! I passed my driving test on the first attempt.”

**Enrollment / Contact Form**
Allow visitors to submit their details.

Fields:

* Full Name
* Phone Number
* Email
* Course Interested
* Preferred Time
* Message

Submit button: **Enroll Now**

After submission show confirmation message:
“Thank you! We will contact you soon.”

**Call to Action Section**
Promotional banner encouraging users to join.

Example text:
“Start Your Driving Journey Today”

CTA button: *Book Your First Lesson*

Background should contain road or driving illustrations.

**Footer**
Include:

* institute description
* quick links (Home, Courses, Gallery, Contact)
* contact details (phone, email, location)
* social media icons (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp)

Footer background should use dark green.

### UI Design Guidelines

* Use rounded cards and soft shadows
* Maintain consistent spacing and typography
* Use driving-related icons and illustrations
* Include subtle animations such as hover effects, fade-in sections, and smooth scrolling

### Technical Requirements

* Built with Next.js and React
* Styled using Tailwind CSS
* Fully responsive (mobile-first)
* Optimized images and fast loading
* SEO friendly
